# Pilot Limitations Disclosure

## Mandatory Disclosure

The controlled commercial pilot must be described with the following limitations intact:

- all bid submission remains human-controlled
- human submission remains mandatory
- the repository provides no legal guarantee
- the repository performs no autonomous platform action
- the repository performs no supplier outreach without explicit human approval outside the repository
- final legal and commercial decisions remain human-only

## Additional Limitations

- pilot outputs are decision-support artifacts, not binding commitments
- quote/TKP ingestion remains manual
- production auth and SaaS hardening are not part of this pilot
- customer-facing materials may include controlled LLM-assisted sections, but they remain schema-validated and review-bound

## Wording To Avoid

- "automatic submission"
- "self-serve tender robot"
- "fully autonomous bidding"
- "legal-safe by default"
- "supplier outreach on autopilot"
