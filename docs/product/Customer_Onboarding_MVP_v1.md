# Customer Onboarding MVP v1

## Positioning

Commercial MVP v1 is a controlled pre-bid support product, not an autonomous tender robot.

It helps:

- structure tender materials
- highlight requirements and risks
- prepare internal commercial decision support
- assemble a human-reviewed bid-readiness package

It does not:

- submit bids
- sign documents
- email suppliers automatically
- replace legal or commercial final approval

## Minimum Customer Inputs

- tender notice text or files
- technical specification
- draft contract if available
- customer-specific qualification assumptions
- operator contact for manual review

## Recommended Onboarding Steps

1. Confirm the use case is pre-bid support only.
2. Confirm the customer accepts manual-control and human-review gates.
3. Collect one representative tender package.
4. Run the bounded MVP demo flow in deterministic or stub-LLM mode.
5. Review the report together with the operator.
6. Agree on what remains manual outside the repository.

## Customer-Facing Talking Points

- traceable recommendations instead of black-box automation
- explicit human control on all commercial decisions
- no risky external execution in MVP v1
- reproducible demo flow for pilot review
- controlled pilot materials include a one-pager, limitations disclosure, pricing test menu, and sample customer report
