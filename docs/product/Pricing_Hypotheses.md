# Pricing Hypotheses

## MVP v1 Commercial Assumption

The first paid pilot should be sold as a controlled workflow package, not as autonomous procurement automation.

## Early Hypotheses

- setup fee for pilot onboarding and operator training
- per-pilot-wave pricing for a bounded number of tenders
- optional premium for controlled LLM-assisted analysis if the customer wants it
- explicit exclusions for submission, EDS, legal sign-off, and supplier outreach automation

## Pricing Risks

- overpromising autonomy would create delivery and compliance risk
- underpricing operator effort would hide the true service cost
- production hardening and auth are still outside MVP v1 scope
