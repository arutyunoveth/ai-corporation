# Sales One-Pager MVP v1

## What It Is

Commercial MVP v1 is an operator-assisted pre-bid support workflow for controlled commercial pilots.

It helps teams:

- structure tender materials quickly
- extract requirements and risks
- prepare manual TKP and economics review
- assemble a human-reviewed bid-readiness package

## What Makes It Safe

- all recommendations stay human-reviewable
- no autonomous bid submission
- no procurement platform actions
- no EDS/signature handling
- no supplier outreach without explicit approval outside the repository

## Why Customers Care

- faster first-pass tender understanding
- clearer risk visibility before bid drafting
- better internal explainability for go/no-go conversations
- reusable operator workflow with audit traces

## Best Fit

- controlled pilot with one operator-led tender at a time
- customers who accept manual-control positioning
- teams that want decision support, not black-box execution

## Honest Positioning

This is not an autonomous tender robot and should not be sold as one.
